Brewing Tips

To get the perfect cup of chai:

  • Use fresh cold water and bring it to a boil.
  • Add 1-2 tsp of Aapani Cha Pati per cup.
  • Simmer for 3-5 minutes for strong flavor.
  • Mix with milk and spices as desired.
  • Strain before serving to enjoy the smooth, rich taste.
